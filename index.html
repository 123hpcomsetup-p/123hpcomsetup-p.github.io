How to reinstall HP wireless printer?


  1. Open "Devices and Printers."
  2. Right-click on your HP printer software and choose "Delete or Remove."
  3. Press "Windows" + "R" keys, type "printui.exe /s," and click "Ok."
  4. In "HP Printer Driver," click "Remove."
  5. Remove all other HP printer software instances.
  6. Restart your PC.
  7. Download and reinstall HP printer software from 123.hp.com/setup.
